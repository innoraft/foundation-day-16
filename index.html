Retracing

1 The Beginning

Started Innoraft in 2011 with 3 members in the team. We had 2 projects - both very small. To start small is the best start, you see yourself grow. Our only goal was to earn enough to fuel the next year.

2 Growth

Team grew from 3 to 10. Projects grew from 2 to 5. Lines of code grew 10x. Location grew to one more. The real growth was in getting more done in less time. Clients appreciated. We became more confident of ourselves.

 

3 Success Stories

Launching some big sites with killer traffic. The launches were not smooth initially but when things ironed out, it was definitely the making of a success story. We started taking pride in our work.

 

4 Managed Hosting

We started managing hosting for our customers and save them big dollars. From vendor, we became a true partner. We were able to handle more traffic with less resources.

 

5 Big Leap

Moved to a 5 times bigger space. The team is now 32. If you have to reach new heights, you have to take the leap of faith. We are expanding in all directions and wish to do more than websites in the coming years.
