#1 the beginning

to start small is the best start, you see yourself grow

Started Innoraft in 2011 with 3 members and 2 moderately small projects. Our only goal was to earn enough to fuel the next year.

 
 

#2 growth

growth is never by mere chance, it is a result of forces working together

Team grew from 3 to 10. Projects grew from 2 to 5. Lines of code grew 10x. Location grew to one more. We were doing more in less time. Clients appreciated and we gained more confidence.

#3 the success stories

work hard in silence. let your success be your noise

Launched some big and complex websites with killer traffic. The launches were not smooth initially but when things ironed out, it was definitely the making of many a success stories. We started taking pride in our work.

 
 

#4 vendors to partners

friendship is essentially a partnership

We started our new service - Managed Hosting managing hosting for our customers and saved them big dollars. From vendor, we became a true partner. We were able to handle more traffic with less resources. Winning our first award - the “Effectiveness in Professional Group” Award from DMA Echo Asia was a big moment.

#5 the big leap

if your dreams don’t scare you, they aren’t big enough

Moved to a 5 times bigger space. The team is now 32. We are expanding in all directions and will be doing lot more more than websites in the coming years!
